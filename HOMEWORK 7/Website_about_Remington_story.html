A Cool Story - The Way of Shadows

The Way of Shadows is a fantasy novel written by Brent Weeks and is the first novel in The Night Angel Trilogy. The story takes place in Cenaria City, the capital of Cenaria, which is located in the fictional land of Midcyru. It centers upon Azoth, a guild rat who seeks an apprenticeship with Durzo Blint, the city's most accomplished wetboy.
